Structure Topic sentence: The first sentence of a paragraph, which states the main idea Supporting sentences: The sentences that explain or prove the topic sentence Concluding sentence: The final sentence of a paragraph, which summarizes the main idea Tips Use transition words to connect ideas and help readers follow your train of thought Be consistent with point of view, verb tense, and number Use parallel structures to create phrases or sentences with the same grammatical structure Repeat key words or phrases to help readers understand the main idea Organize paragraphs spatially, in order of appearance, or by topic Use examples to illustrate your point Purpose
